Smart Security Gateway - FAQ

Common questions about the free service
Is this service really free?
Yes. It is a free, research-grade security layer with fair-use limits.
How do I protect my website?
Point your domain to the gateway IP, and keep your origin server private.
Do you use image CAPTCHAs?
No. The system relies on behavior signals and invisible challenges.
What data is collected?
Behavioral signals and technical request metadata (see Privacy Policy).
How long do you keep data?
Behavioral data for 7 days by default, reports up to 30 days.
Can I request higher limits?
Yes, if you have research or educational justification. Contact support.
Where can I read the policies?
See Fair Use, Privacy, and Terms.
