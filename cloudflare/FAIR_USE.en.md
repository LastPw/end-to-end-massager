Fair Use Policy (Free Tier)

Purpose
This service is free and should be shared fairly among all users. This policy prevents abuse and protects service quality.

Default Limits
- Up to 1,000,000 requests per month per domain
- Up to 100 requests per minute per IP (limited tuning available)
- Log retention up to 30 days (7 days recommended)
- Resource cap: 1 GB for behavioral data and reports
- Maximum domains per account: 3

Prohibited Use
- Hosting or protecting illegal content
- Using the service to launch DDoS or unauthorized security testing
- Attempting to bypass service limits

Enforcement
- Exceeding limits: temporary throttling or suspension for the domain
- Repeated abuse: account suspension or domain removal

Contact
Requests for higher limits are considered only for documented research or educational use.
